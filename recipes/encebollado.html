Encebollado (Ecuadorian Fish Soup)

A fish soup that would satisfy your stomach!

Encebollado is a fish soup that is the most popular soup in all of Ecuador. It is an absolute must-have for those interested in trying Ecuadorian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs tuna
  • 1 lb yuca
  • 2 tbs sunflower oil
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 red onion (diced)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 8 cups of water
  • 5 ciliantro sprigs
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil on medium heat to make a refrito with diced onion, cumin, chili powder and salt.
  2. Add the water and cilantro springs, bring to a boil.
  3. Add the tuna and cook for until the tuna is fully cooked, about 15 minutes.
  4. Drain the tuna and keep the broth to cook the yuca.
  5. Separate or break the tuna into small to medium size pieces.
  6. Bring the tuna broth to a boil and add the yucas, cook until tender but firm, about 30-40 minutes for the frozen yuca.
  7. Take the yuca from the broth, remove the strings and cut into bite size chunks.
  8. Add the yuca chunks and tuna pieces to the broth, taste and add salt if needed.
  9. Serve topped with a good amount of pickled onion and tomato salsa, lime and plantain chips.
